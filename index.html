How a sub-score is calculated
Higher-is-better (AJS, NPS, Reviews): min(100, value ÷ standard × 100)
Lower-is-better (Complaints): min(100, standard ÷ value × 100)
Example. A BNO TM with AJS $580 (standard $725): 580 ÷ 725 × 100 = 80. That sub-score then weighs 50% in the composite.
