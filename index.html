    How a metric scores
    Hit the standard: full weight (50, 20, 15, or 15)
    Miss the standard: 0
    Example. A BNO TM hits AJS, hits NPS, hits Reviews, misses Complaints: 50 + 0 + 15 + 15 = 80.
    Severity buckets
    Urgent · score < 65 (missing AJS or 2+ standards) High · 65–79 (hits AJS but missing 1-2 secondaries) Medium · 80+ (hits 3+ of 4 standards)
